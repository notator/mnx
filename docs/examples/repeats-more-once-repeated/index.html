MNX example: Repeats (more than once repeated)

In this example, the measure is to be performed four times (or “repeated three times,” depending on your perspective).

Concepts: Repeats

See also: This same example in MusicXML

<mnx> <global> <measure> <directions> <time signature="4/4"/> <repeat type="end" times="4"/> </directions> </measure> </global> <part> <part-name> Music </part-name> <measure> <directions> <clef sign="G" line="2"/> </directions> <sequence> <event value="/1"> <note pitch="C5"/> </event> </sequence> </measure> </part> </mnx>
